# Privacy Policy

Boswell Consulting Group

Version: 1.0  
Prepared: 2026-06-11

## Overview

Boswell Consulting Group collects information needed to provide administrative royalty-registration support for independent recording artists.

## Information Collected

Information may include:

- Name
- Artist/stage name
- Email
- Phone number
- Country and state/province
- Song titles
- Release metadata
- Writer, producer, publisher, and ownership details
- ISRCs and UPCs
- Distributor details
- Store links
- Split sheets or agreements
- Portal confirmation files
- Payment confirmation details

## How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

- Review work-order intake
- Identify missing information
- Perform authorized administrative portal support
- Track work-order status
- Send client updates
- Store completion reports and confirmation references
- Maintain internal business records

## Payment Information

Payments are processed through Stripe. Boswell Consulting Group does not store full card numbers.

## Credentials

Passwords should not be submitted through email or open form fields. If portal access is required, use a secure password manager, screen-share login, or another approved secure workflow.

## File Storage

Client files may be stored in secure business cloud storage for the purpose of completing and documenting the work order.

## Sharing

Boswell Consulting Group does not sell client information. Information may be used with selected royalty portals only as needed to complete authorized administrative work.

## Retention

Client work-order records may be retained for business, support, and documentation purposes unless deletion is requested and retention is not required for legitimate business or legal reasons.
