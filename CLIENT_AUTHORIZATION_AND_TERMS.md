# Client Authorization and Service Terms

Boswell Consulting Group

Version: 1.0  
Prepared: 2026-06-11

## Service Description

Boswell Consulting Group provides administrative royalty-registration support for independent recording artists. Services may include intake review, metadata organization, registration or claim support for BMI or ASCAP, The MLC, and SoundExchange, work-order tracking, confirmation documentation, and follow-up reminders.

## No Guarantee

Boswell Consulting Group does not guarantee royalties, income, registration acceptance, claim approval, collection, matching, portal processing speed, tax treatment, legal ownership, or any specific financial result.

## Not Professional Advice

This service is not legal, tax, accounting, financial, publishing, business-management, or investment advice. Artists should consult qualified professionals for questions about ownership disputes, contracts, taxes, publishing administration, legal rights, business formation, or royalty entitlement.

## Artist Responsibility

The artist is responsible for:

- Providing accurate song, recording, ownership, and split information.
- Confirming they have the right to request work on the listed songs and recordings.
- Resolving disputes with collaborators.
- Reviewing all portal submissions and completion reports.
- Paying official portal fees, if any.
- Maintaining their own portal accounts, payment details, tax forms, and passwords.

## Limited Authorization

By submitting a paid work order, the artist authorizes Boswell Consulting Group to perform administrative support for the selected work order only. This authorization does not transfer ownership, publishing, master rights, royalty rights, account ownership, or payment rights to Boswell Consulting Group.

## Portal Credentials

Passwords should not be sent by normal email or open text fields. If portal access is required, the artist should use a secure password manager, screen-share login, or another approved secure workflow. Boswell Consulting Group should not store unnecessary credentials after the work is complete.

## Official Fees

Official portal fees, if any, are not included unless stated in writing. The artist should pay official fees directly whenever possible.

## Refunds

Recommended policy:

- Full refund before intake review begins.
- No refund after intake review begins.
- Work orders missing required artist information may remain open for 30 days.
- If the artist does not provide required missing information within 30 days, the work order may be closed.

## Disputes and Blocked Work

Boswell Consulting Group may pause or decline work if there are unresolved ownership disputes, conflicting split claims, missing account access, suspected fraud, unauthorized claims, incomplete data, or portal requirements that cannot be satisfied.

## Completion Standard

A work order is complete when Boswell Consulting Group provides a completion report showing the portal actions taken, confirmation references available, unresolved items, and recommended follow-up dates.
