Independent Artist Royalty Registration Portal by Boswell Consulting Group is a done-with-you administrative service for independent recording artists who need help organizing and submitting royalty-registration work orders for BMI or ASCAP, The MLC, and SoundExchange.

Artists submit song, release, split, metadata, and portal-access details through a work order. Boswell Consulting Group reviews the intake, identifies missing information, completes selected administrative registration or claim tasks where authorized, and returns a completion summary with confirmations and follow-up items.

This is administrative support only. No royalties, income, legal result, tax result, ownership result, claim approval, portal acceptance, or collection outcome is guaranteed.
