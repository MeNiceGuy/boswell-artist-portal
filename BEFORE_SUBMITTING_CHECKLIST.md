# Before Submitting a Work Order

Use this checklist before purchasing or submitting a royalty-registration work order.

## Artist Details

- Legal name
- Artist/stage name
- Email
- Phone
- Country and state/province

## Portal Status

- BMI or ASCAP selected
- BMI or ASCAP login status known
- The MLC account status known
- SoundExchange account status known
- Any existing registrations saved

## Song or Release Details

- Song title
- Recording title, if different
- Primary artist
- Featured artists
- Writers
- Producers
- Release date
- Distributor
- UPC
- ISRC
- Store links

## Ownership and Splits

- Writer splits
- Publisher splits
- Master ownership
- Producer agreement status
- Featured artist agreement status
- Any samples disclosed
- Any cover song disclosed
- Any unresolved disputes disclosed

## Files to Upload

- Split sheet
- Producer agreement, if applicable
- Featured artist agreement, if applicable
- Distributor metadata screenshot
- ISRC/UPC screenshot
- Prior portal confirmations
- Store links or release proof

## Important

Do not submit passwords through email or open form fields. Do not submit a work order if you know there is an unresolved ownership dispute and you are not willing to disclose it.
