# Credential Policy

Boswell Consulting Group

Version: 1.0  
Prepared: 2026-06-11

## Core Rule

Clients must not send passwords through normal email, text message, social media DM, or open intake form fields.

## Approved Access Methods

Use one of these methods if portal access is required:

- Client logs in during a live screen-share session.
- Client shares access through a reputable password manager.
- Client creates limited user access if the portal supports it.
- Client performs the final login/submission while Boswell Consulting Group provides administrative instructions.

## Storage

Boswell Consulting Group should not store unnecessary client credentials. If temporary access is needed, remove or revoke it after the work order is complete.

## Payment and Tax Details

Clients are responsible for their own payment, banking, and tax information inside official portals. Boswell Consulting Group should not change payment destination, tax forms, or ownership claims unless the client has specifically authorized the administrative action and understands what is being changed.

## Sensitive Documents

Sensitive documents should be uploaded only through the approved secure intake or file-upload system.
