Ad Copy and Creative Brief

Audience:
Independent recording artists with released or soon-to-be-released music who need help organizing royalty-registration tasks.

Primary text:
Released music but not sure your royalty registrations are handled? Submit a work order and get administrative support for BMI or ASCAP, The MLC, and SoundExchange.

Alternate primary text:
Your song metadata, splits, ISRCs, UPCs, and portal registrations should not live in scattered screenshots. Get a clean work-order process for royalty-registration support.

Headline options:
- Submit a royalty registration work order
- Get your music admin organized
- Royalty portal support for independent artists
- BMI or ASCAP, The MLC, and SoundExchange support

Offer angle:
Starter Release Bundle - $97 for account setup checklist, up to 12 songs, completion tracker, and 30-day follow-up. Additional tracks are $5 each.

Creative direction:
Use clean studio, laptop, checklist, portal dashboard, folder, calendar, and tracker visuals. Avoid fake earnings screenshots, cash imagery, streaming-income promises, or misleading royalty claims.

Compliance:
Do not promise royalties, income, legal protection, tax outcomes, portal approval, matching, collection, or guaranteed results.
