# Pricing and Packages

Boswell Consulting Group

Version: 1.0  
Prepared: 2026-06-11

## Pricing Principle

Charge for administrative labor, intake review, portal submission support, documentation, and follow-up. Do not bundle official portal fees into the headline price unless you are intentionally collecting pass-through fees. Official fees, if any, should be billed separately or paid directly by the artist.

## Recommended Small-Artist Launch Pricing

### 1. Royalty Account Setup

Price: $49 one-time

For artists who need their core royalty accounts organized before work registrations begin.

Includes:

- One artist/admin profile review.
- BMI or ASCAP affiliation readiness checklist.
- The MLC membership/readiness checklist.
- SoundExchange registration/readiness checklist.
- Admin folder setup instructions.
- One missing-information report.

### 2. Single Song Registration Work Order

Price: $29 per song

For one song or single release.

Includes:

- Intake review for one composition and one sound recording.
- BMI or ASCAP work registration support for the selected PRO.
- The MLC work registration or claim support when applicable.
- SoundExchange recording claim or catalog support when applicable.
- Completion summary with confirmations and unresolved items.

Recommended turnaround: 5 business days after complete intake.

### 3. Release Pack

Price: $97 per release, up to 12 songs

For EPs, albums, or multi-song release batches.

Includes:

- Intake review for up to 12 songs.
- Metadata cleanup checklist.
- Selected PRO registration support.
- The MLC registration/claim support.
- SoundExchange catalog/recording claim support.
- Completion summary for each song.
- 30-day follow-up check.

Additional tracks: $5 each.

### 4. Catalog Cleanup Sprint

Price: $200, up to 25 older songs

For artists with older releases, inconsistent metadata, or missing royalty registrations.

Includes:

- Catalog audit for up to 25 older songs.
- Missing-information report.
- Priority registration queue.
- Selected PRO, The MLC, and SoundExchange support where applicable.
- Confirmation tracker.
- 30-day and 90-day follow-up check.

Additional older songs beyond 25 require a custom quote.

### 5. Monthly Royalty Admin Desk

Price: $49/month

For active artists releasing consistently.

Includes:

- Up to 2 single-song work orders per month.
- Quarterly royalty-registration audit.
- Work-order tracker access or monthly summary.
- Priority email support.
- Discounted extra songs at $12 per song.

Minimum term: 3 months.

## Add-Ons

- Metadata rebuild report: $49.
- Confirmation archive cleanup: $49.
- Quarterly audit only: $49.

## Suggested Launch Offer

Starter Release Bundle: $97

Includes:

- Royalty Account Setup checklist.
- Up to 12 songs in one Release Pack.
- 30-day follow-up.
- Completion tracker.

This should be the main entry offer for smaller artists. Keep the higher Catalog Cleanup Sprint available for artists with larger back catalogs.

## Refund Policy Recommendation

- Full refund before intake review begins.
- No refund after intake review begins, because administrative labor has started.
- If Boswell Consulting Group cannot complete a work order because of missing artist information, the order remains open for 30 days.
- If the artist does not provide missing information within 30 days, the order may be closed without refund.

## Payment Language

Payment covers administrative service labor only. Official portal fees, if any, are not included unless clearly stated on the invoice. No royalty payment, income amount, claim approval, registration acceptance, or collection outcome is guaranteed.
