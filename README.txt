Independent Artist Royalty Registration Portal
Boswell Consulting Group

This product is now positioned as a service portal for independent recording artists who want Boswell Consulting Group to organize and submit royalty-registration work orders for BMI or ASCAP, The MLC, and SoundExchange.

Start here:
1. Open PORTAL_SERVICE_BLUEPRINT.md to understand the offer.
2. Open PRICING_AND_PACKAGES.md to review the recommended pricing model.
3. Open WORK_ORDER_INTAKE_FORM.md to see the intake fields artists must complete.
4. Open CLIENT_AUTHORIZATION_AND_TERMS.md before accepting paid work.
5. Open STRIPE_PAYMENT_LINK_SETUP.md and create the Stripe links.
6. Open SECURE_INTAKE_FORM_BUILD.md and build the live intake form.
7. Paste the live URLs into LIVE_LINKS_TO_REPLACE.md.
8. Replace the Stripe and intake placeholders inside ARTIST_PORTAL_MVP.html.
9. Open ADMIN_WORK_ORDER_TRACKER.csv or import it into Airtable/Google Sheets.
10. Open ARTIST_PORTAL_MVP.html in a browser for the no-backend portal prototype.

The original guide and tracker are still included as buyer/admin support assets.
