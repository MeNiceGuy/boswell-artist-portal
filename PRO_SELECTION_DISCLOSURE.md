# BMI vs ASCAP Selection Disclosure

Boswell Consulting Group

Version: 1.0  
Prepared: 2026-06-11

## Core Rule

The artist is responsible for choosing BMI or ASCAP. Boswell Consulting Group can provide an administrative checklist and help organize information, but it does not choose a PRO for the artist as legal, financial, or career advice.

## Why This Matters

BMI and ASCAP are separate performing rights organizations. A writer generally should not submit the same writer share for the same work to both BMI and ASCAP. Duplicate or conflicting registrations can create confusion, delays, or disputes.

## Intake Question

Ask every artist:

Which PRO are you affiliated with or choosing for this work order?

Options:

- BMI
- ASCAP
- I am already affiliated but need to confirm which one
- I am not affiliated yet
- I need to pause and decide before work starts

## Client Acknowledgment

Required checkbox:

I understand that I am responsible for selecting my PRO. Boswell Consulting Group is not choosing BMI or ASCAP for me and is not providing legal, financial, publishing, or career advice about which PRO I should join.

## Blocked Work

If the artist cannot confirm their PRO selection, move the work order to "Needs Artist Info" until the artist decides.
