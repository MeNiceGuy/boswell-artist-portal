Sources and References

Last checked: 2026-06-11

Use these official sources before submitting artist registration, claim, account, tax, or payment information. Portal workflows, fees, eligibility rules, and required fields can change.

Core royalty-registration portals:

- BMI Join: https://www.bmi.com/join
  Notes checked 2026-06-11: BMI lists separate songwriter and publisher affiliation paths. BMI states writer applicants sign a 2-year agreement. BMI states publisher applicants sign a 5-year agreement and need a credit card for a one-time processing fee.

- ASCAP official site: https://www.ascap.com/
- ASCAP join: https://www.ascap.com/join
- ASCAP repertory/Songview: https://www.ascap.com/repertory

- The MLC Membership: https://www.themlc.com/membership
  Notes checked 2026-06-11: The MLC says self-administered independent songwriters can become members to register works, suggest matches, and collect eligible U.S. digital audio mechanical royalties. The MLC says joining is free.

- The MLC official site: https://www.themlc.com/
- The MLC FAQ: https://www.themlc.com/faqs/categories/mlc
- The MLC Missing Member Lookup: https://www.themlc.com/missing-member-lookup
- The MLC Public Search: https://portal.themlc.com/search

- SoundExchange Register: https://www.soundexchange.com/register/
  Notes checked 2026-06-11: SoundExchange says creator/copyright-holder registration is free, simple, and fast. SoundExchange states SoundExchange Direct lets registered creators claim recordings, manage catalog data, and review digital royalty payments.

- SoundExchange registration portal: https://register.soundexchange.com/
- SoundExchange Direct: https://sxdirect.soundexchange.com/
- SoundExchange who we are: https://www.soundexchange.com/who-we-are/
