FAQ:
Do you guarantee royalties?
No. This is administrative support only. Royalties depend on eligibility, usage, matching, portal rules, account status, ownership, and other factors.

Do you choose BMI or ASCAP for me?
No. The artist chooses their PRO. Boswell Consulting Group can provide an admin checklist, but the choice is the artist's responsibility.

Do you collect my royalties?
No. The artist keeps ownership, account control, and payment destination.

Do official portal fees come out of the package price?
No. Official fees, if any, are separate unless clearly stated in writing.
