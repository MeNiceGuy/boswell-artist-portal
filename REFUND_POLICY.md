# Refund Policy

Boswell Consulting Group

Version: 1.0  
Prepared: 2026-06-11

## General Rule

Payment covers administrative service labor. Refunds depend on whether work has started.

## Full Refund

A full refund may be available if:

- The client requests cancellation before intake review begins.
- The client accidentally purchases the wrong package and contacts Boswell Consulting Group before work begins.

## No Refund After Work Starts

No refund is provided after intake review begins because administrative labor has started.

## Missing Information

If required information is missing, the work order may be moved to "Needs Artist Info."

The client has 30 days to provide missing information. If the client does not provide the required information within 30 days, the work order may be closed without refund.

## Blocked or Disputed Work

Boswell Consulting Group may pause or decline work if there are unresolved split disputes, ownership conflicts, suspected fraud, missing portal access, or incomplete authorization.

## Official Fees

Official portal fees, if any, are separate and are not included unless clearly stated in writing. Boswell Consulting Group does not refund fees paid directly to third-party portals.

## No Guarantee

Refunds are not issued because a portal does not approve, match, accept, process, or pay royalties on a work. No royalty payment, income, claim approval, matching, or collection outcome is guaranteed.
